01 / CASE STUDY

VISUAL
LANGUAGE

High-contrast structural systems for the modern web.

Focusing on high-contrast visual systems and brutalist architecture for the web. An exploration into how structural rigidity can coexist with digital fluidity.

Fig. 01 / Portrait 2024 / Studio v.24
ROLE Lead Designer
TOOLS USED Figma, Webflow
TIMELINE 12 WEEKS
CLIENT AXON STUDIO

THE
PROBLEM

Conventional digital interfaces had become soft and predictable. The brand needed to break away from rounded "friendly" aesthetics to command authority in the architectural space.

THE
APPROACH

We implemented a strict 12-column grid system inspired by Swiss typography. Every element was stripped to its core essence—eliminating shadows, radii, and unnecessary ornamentation.

Color was used as a functional tool rather than decoration. The electric blue serves as the singular beacon for user interaction points.

THE
SOLUTION

A monolith digital experience that feels as permanent as concrete. The resulting system saw a 40% increase in user engagement due to the clarity and hierarchy of information.
